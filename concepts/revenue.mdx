Unless a field is explicitly labeled TTM (trailing twelve months) or yearly, treat money as **monthly USD**.

## Converting yearly → monthly

| User says | Use |
| --------- | --- |
| \$5M / year | `min_revenue=416667` |
| \$10M / year | `min_revenue=833333` |
| \$1M / month | `min_revenue=1000000` |
| \$500K–\$5M / year | `min_revenue=41667`, `max_revenue=416667` |

## Where it appears

- `search_brands` — `min_revenue` / `max_revenue` are monthly
- Brand profile — monthly revenue, MoM/YoY growth, TTM when provided
- Products — monthly revenue estimates
- Categories — total monthly revenue for the subcategory

## Coverage when browsing

Category brand searches scan product pages under the hood. Responses include a `coverage` object:

| Field | Meaning |
| ----- | ------- |
| `products_scanned` | How many products were scanned |
| `unique_brands_found` | Distinct brands after filters |
| `has_more` | More pages available |
| `continue_with` | Suggested `start_page` for the next call |

Increase `scan_pages` (default 20, max 50) or page with `start_page` until `has_more` is false or new brands taper off.
